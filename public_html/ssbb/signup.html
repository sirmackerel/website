Please complete the above form and hit submit if you plan on participating in either the singles or the doubles tourney. You are allowed to participate in both!

On the day of the tournament, please arrive before 7:15pm to check in for the tournament to verify you are here. You may also sign up at this time if you haven't already. Matches are expected to start at 7:30pm, so if you haven't signed up and checked in by then, you will be unable to do so once matches start.

If you would like to play doubles but do not have a partner, just leave the 'doubles partner's name' field blank and we will try to pair you up with someone else without a partner. However, if you are the odd man out and there is no one left to pair you with, then tough luck (sorry!).
If your doubles partner would also like to play in the singles tournament, he or she must also fill in and submit the sign up form!

Feel free to send any questions to bacchus@stwing.upenn.edu.
